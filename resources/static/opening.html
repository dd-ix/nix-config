DD-IX Opening

After a year of work, we want to celebrate!

We cordially invite you to the opening of DD-IX, the first public Internet Exchange in Dresden, Saxony. Join us for a recap of the last year, community talks, food and drinks. Participation is free but we kindly ask you to register. Let’s celebrate together!
